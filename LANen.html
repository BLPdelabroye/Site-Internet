What is a LAN Party?

A LAN party is an event where friends get together to play video games on a local network. The venue is usually someone's living room or garage. It's the perfect opportunity to get together with friends, and enjoy an activity that everyone loves. LAN parties are all about creating memories unforgettable‚ù§Ô∏è.

Why participate in a LAN Party?

  • Local multiplayer: Playing with your friends in the same room is much more fun!
  • Friendly competition: Test your skills face-to-face with your friends.
  • Improved communication: Team strategy, fast and effective communication.
  • Friendly atmosphere: Snacks, laughter, and a craziness between games.

Popular games at LAN Parties

Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO)

A tactical first-person shooter in 5v5 where teams (Terrorists vs. Counter-Terrorists) compete to plant or defuse a bomb. Skillful aiming, quick reflexes, and team strategy are essential to securing victory.

League of Legends (LoL)

A competitive MOBA in 5v5 where teams battle to destroy the enemy Nexus. Players select from a wide roster of champions with unique abilities, working together to control the map and overpower their opponents.

Valorant

A tactical team-based shooter (5v5) where strategy and precision are key. Each player takes on the role of an agent with unique abilities, adding a strategic layer to fast-paced and intense firefights.

Minecraft

A creative sandbox game where players can explore randomly generated worlds, gather resources, build structures, and either cooperate or compete in different game modes, from survival to custom mini-games.

Tips for organizing a LAN party

  • Prepare your games and updates in advance. And if you want to record your screens, take video-capture devices with you.
  • Plan the place where your LAN will take place, make sure there's enough room, and make sure there are relaxation areas for those who want to take a break.
  • Bring your own equipment (screen, PC, peripherals)
  • Have enough Ethernet cables, extension cords, switches and multiple sockets.
  • Pack snacks and drinks to keep you going all night!
